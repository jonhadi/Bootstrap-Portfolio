About Me

I’ve always loved learning how things work. I remember asking the question, “Why?” until my parents ran out of answers. Growing up, I played with Legos, and when I would get a new set; I follow the instructions, build it once, tear it apart, and then incorporate it into my own design. This curiosity has continued into my adulthood. I love dissecting processes, understanding, and enhancing them. If I can safely do it myself, then I am trying to do it myself. This mindset has led me into many jobs and hobbies. I’ve served in a Thai, Japanese, Korean, and American restaurant, became a demolitionist, tutored students, and now supporting dozens of clients as a System Support Specialist. My passion for technology originated from my main hobby of playing videogames. It started with just playing games, then hosting servers for my friends to play together, then into modding games. This led me to taking classes and certifications and learning about the ins and out of supporting a company. I felt the next step, becoming a web developer is the perfect fit. There is always something to learn because it is always evolving, but most of all it brings together imagination, analysis, and most of all creating.
